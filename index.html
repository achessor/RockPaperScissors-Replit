Let's play: Rock Paper Scissors!

Also known as "Roshambo", originated in China and is a game between 2 people (or player vs computer). Player will choose between rock, paper, or scissors and the computer will randomly select rock, paper, or scissors. If both players select the same item (rock v rock, paper v paper, scissors v scissors) is is a tie, nobody wins. But the game rules go like this for other options:
Rock beats Scissors. Scissors beats Paper. Paper beats Rock.
